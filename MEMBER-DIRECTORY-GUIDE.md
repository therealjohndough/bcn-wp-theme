## 📝 How to Add a New Member (Step-by-Step)

### Option 1: Using BCN Dashboard (Easiest)

1. **Log into WordPress Admin**
2. **Go to**: BCN Dashboard → Add New Member
   - OR: Click "BCN Members" in sidebar → Add New

3. **Fill in Member Details:**

   **Title Field** (top):
   - Enter: Member's business name (e.g., "DRS Testing")

   **Featured Image** (right sidebar):
   - Click "Set featured image"
   - Upload company logo (PNG or WebP preferred)
   - Recommended size: 400x200px or similar
   - Click "Set featured image"

   **Member Details Meta Box** (below editor):
   - 📝 **Company Name**: Official business name
   - 🌐 **Website URL**: https://example.com (creates backlink!)
   - 👤 **Contact Name**: Primary contact person
   - ✉️ **Email**: Contact email
   - 📞 **Phone**: Business phone number
   - 🏢 **Industry**: e.g., "Testing Lab", "Dispensary", "Consulting"
   - 📅 **Join Date**: When they became a member
   - ⭐ **Featured Member**: Check to highlight on homepage

   **Membership Tier** (right sidebar):
   - Select: Premier Member, Professional Member, or Student Member

   **Content Area** (optional):
   - Add company description, services, etc.
   - This appears on their individual member page

4. **Click "Publish"**

**That's it!** The member will automatically appear:
- ✨ On the homepage (in their tier section)
- 📋 In the member directory (`/members`)
- 🔗 With backlink to their website
- 📊 With schema markup for SEO

   - Link members to blog posts they contributed

4. **Regional Authority**:
   - Shows you're connected with Buffalo businesses
   - Builds trust and credibility

## 🎯 Advanced Features

### Link Members to Events

When creating/editing an event:
1. In the event content, mention members who attended
2. Link to their member profile: `/members/company-name/`
3. Example: "Featured speakers from **[DRS Testing](/members/drs-testing/)** and **[Weed Ross](/members/weed-ross/)**"

### Link Members to Blog Posts

When writing blog posts:
1. Tag members who contributed
2. Link to their profile
3. Example: "Special thanks to **[Case Study Labs](/members/case-study-labs/)** for this analysis"

### Add Member Photos from Events

On individual member pages:
1. Edit the member
2. Add gallery in content area
3. Upload photos from events featuring that member
4. Caption: "The DRS Testing team at BCN Brand Showcase 2025"

**SEO Benefit**: Photos with captions create rich content and improve member page rankings!

## 💡 Pro Tips for Maximum SEO

### 1. Write Good Member Descriptions
- 2-3 paragraphs about the company
- Include keywords naturally
- Mention what they offer Buffalo cannabis community

### 2. Get Reciprocal Links
- Ask members to link back to BCN from their sites
- Creates powerful SEO network
- Benefits everyone!

### 3. Regular Updates
- Add new members as they join
- Update logos/info when companies rebrand
- Add event photos to member pages

### 4. Content Strategy
- **Blog posts**: Interview members, spotlight their work
- **Event recaps**: Feature members who attended
- **Case studies**: Show how BCN helped members succeed

### 5. Internal Linking
- Link from events to members
- Link from blog posts to members
- Link from member pages to events they attended
- Creates strong site architecture (Google loves this!)

**Great idea!** Here's how:

- Internal links strengthen site structure
- Shows activity and community engagement
